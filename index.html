Clipbit is a clipboard manager for macOS that gives you instant access to your copy history. Its unique circular pie-menu interface makes selecting past clips intuitive and lightning-fast.

â“ FAQ

Which macOS versions are supported?

Clipbit supports macOS 14 (Sonoma) and later.

Where is my clipboard history stored?

All data is stored locally on your Mac. Nothing is ever sent to external servers.

How do I open the pie menu?

Use the keyboard shortcut you configure in settings to summon the pie menu anytime, anywhere.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility and make sure Clipbit has permission. If the issue persists, please contact us.
